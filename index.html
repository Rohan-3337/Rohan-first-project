    anti sleep alarm

    The "anti sleep alarm(earpiece)" is a driver safety device that is designed to help prevent drowsy driving. It uses advanced sensors to detect when the driver is nodding off, and then sounds an alarm to wake them up. The device is compact and easy to use, simply attach it to the visor in the car. Its small, discreet design allows it to be used in any vehicle, and it can be easily turned on and off as needed. With the "Buzzer" you can stay alert and safe on the road. It is perfect for long-distance drivers, night shift workers, and anyone who wants to ensure their safety on the road

    anti sleep glasses

    The "anti sleep glasses" is an innovative driver safety device that uses Google Glass technology to detect when the driver is nodding off. With the advanced sensors integrated with it, it will detect the drowsy driving and start buzzing to awake the driver. This innovative device provides a hands-free and distraction-free solution for staying alert on the road. It connects to your smartphone via Bluetooth and can be used in any vehicle. With the "Google Glass," you can stay focused and alert on the road, giving you peace of mind while driving. It's perfect for long-distance drivers, night shift workers, and anyone who wants to ensure their safety on the road.

    About us

    We believe that drowsy driving is a serious issue that can have devastating consequences. Our mission is to raise awareness about the dangers of driving while tired, and to provide practical tips and resources to help prevent accidents caused by sleep-deprived drivers. Whether you're a long-distance trucker, a busy parent, or a college student pulling an all-nighter

    we want to empower you to make safer driving decisions and to arrive at your destination safely and alert
